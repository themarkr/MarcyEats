Our Story
Habanero began 10 years ago with just a dream. As kids we had an idea of creating a place that shared our love for "habanero" chips. This idea became a reality at the beginning of 2018 when an opportunity to start a restaurant as a family opened for us. This restaurant incorporates Mexican seasoning to the ever changing culture we see around us. We are the new Tex-Mex.
